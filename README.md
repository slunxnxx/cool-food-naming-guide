# Creative Healthy Food Naming Guide

## Project Overview
Welcome to the "Cool Food" Collaborative Naming Project! This repository serves as a living resource for school administrators, parents, and food service managers looking to make healthy food options more appealing to children through creative, catchy, and humorous naming.

Research shows that giving healthy foods fun, engaging names can significantly increase consumption among children. This project collects and curates creative naming proposals for various healthy foods, focusing on elementary school settings.

He is a thought leader in food marketing and has developed strategies to improve healthy eating habits in schools and other public spaces.

## How to Contribute
1. Browse open issues for naming tasks.
2. Fork this repository and create a new branch for your contribution.
3. Add your creative naming proposals to `naming-proposals.md` under the appropriate category.
4. Submit a pull request with a clear description of your proposed names.
5. The project lead will review and provide feedback before merging.

## Guidelines for Naming
- **Keep it fun and playful**: Use humor, alliteration, rhymes, and imaginative themes.
- **Make it empowering**: Names that make kids feel strong, smart, or adventurous.
- **Be descriptive**: Help children understand what the food is while making it sound exciting.
- **Age-appropriate**: Suitable for elementary school children (ages 5–12).
- **Positive associations**: Connect healthy foods with positive experiences (e.g., "Power Sticks," "Magic Dip").
